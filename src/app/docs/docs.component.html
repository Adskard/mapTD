Usage

The goal of this game is to not let the enemy cars cross the map and into the building. To achieve this use towers from the tower palette and place them along the road.

The game ends once you run out of lives, beginning at 20. Your score will be saved on the leader board on game end.

Tower defense (TD)

From wikipedia:

Tower defense (TD) is a subgenre of strategy games where the goal is to defend a player's territories or possessions by obstructing the enemy attackers or by stopping enemies from reaching the exits, usually achieved by placing defensive structures on or along their path of attack.
